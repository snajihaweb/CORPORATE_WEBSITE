ENTRY OFFER TO EDUCATION OF SELANGOR ORPHANS (RUMAH BAKTI DATO’ HARUN)

The mother or heir is welcomed to send the orphans under the care of the yours to the RBDH dormitory at any time. Orphans placed in RBDH dormitories will be given placement, religious guidance, guarding, schooling and eating and drinking while here for FREE

HOW TO APPLY FOR ADOPTION

Here are the basics of taking orphans in RBDH:

  • Malaysian citizen

  • Children whose death is father or both (mother and father)

  • Children who are willing to go to school are between 7 to 14 years old

  • Children who are body healthy and not disabled after obtaining a doctoral endorsement

  • Poor families and no closest heirs who are willing to bear it

  • Children who have an interest and sincerity to continue their studies and improve their lives and families in the future.


    • HOW TO APPLY FOR ADMISSION

    The heir takes and fills out the Entry Application Form.

    Forms may be taken at the RBDH Officials or download the form.

    The assessment panel will contact the heir and visit the heir to see for himself the state of the families of these orphans.

    THE ASPECTS THAT ARE GIVEN ATTENTION ARE:

  • Family background

  • The state of residence

  • The economic stage of the family

  • State of the self

  • Health

  • Student schooling

  • Family atmosphere

  • The life environment of these orphans

    • The results of the tactic were laid out in the ration of the Ordination Office for the purpose of ordination and graduation. Contacting the orphans' heirs expressed agreement to accept or reject their requests.
